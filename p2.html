PROMISES

What is Promise?

Promise is built in constructor of javascript

A promise object represents an operation which has produced or will eventually produce a value.Promises provide a robust way to wrap the (possibly pending) result of asynchrous request

States of Promises

1. Pending

The underlying operation has not yet completed or neither rejected

2. Fulfilled

The operation has finished and promise is fulfilled with a response. (.then()) is used handle fulfilled response.

3. Rejected

The error occured during the operation is rejected with a reason. (.catch()) is used to catch the error.

4. Settled

A promise a said to settled,(either resolved or rejected).Once the promise is settled,it becomes imutable.
